Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and products

What is your 20-step eye examination? ▼
Our 20-step eye examination is a comprehensive vision assessment using the latest equipment. It evaluates your complete visual system including refraction, binocular vision, eye health, and early detection of any concerns. Unlike standard tests, it provides accurate prescriptions and identifies potential issues early.
Do you offer online ordering? ▼
Yes! You can order through WhatsApp by sending your prescription and frame preference. We'll guide you through the process, make your glasses with precision, and deliver to your home across Kumaon.
Are your products genuine? ▼
100% Yes! We stock only authentic products from globally trusted brands like Essilor, Zeiss, Ray-Ban, and Johnson & Johnson. All products come with original packaging and warranties.
What warranty do you provide? ▼
All our products come with manufacturer warranty. We also provide warranty on frame adjustment, lens fitting, and after-sales support. Ask us about specific warranty details for your product.
Can I get contact lenses for astigmatism? ▼
Yes! We have Toric contact lenses from leading brands like Acuvue, Air Optix, and more for astigmatism correction. Our optometrists can fit and guide you on proper usage.
Do you have kids' frames? ▼
Yes! We have a special collection of kids' frames in various styles and colors. We also use flexible, durable materials like TR90 for children's eyewear.
What is the delivery time for online orders? ▼
Typically 5-7 days depending on your location within Kumaon and the lens type. Express orders can be arranged. Contact us on WhatsApp for exact timing.
Can I get progressive lenses? ▼
Yes! Progressive lenses are perfect for presbyopia and multifocal needs. We offer premium options from Essilor, Zeiss, Hoya and more with various price points.
