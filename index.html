Process

From exam to fitting, in four visits.

Most opticians rush the middle two. We built the shop around them.

01

Eye test

A 30-minute exam with retinoscopy and auto-refraction. Axis and pupillary distance measured by hand, not guessed.

02

Choose a frame

Try in-store, or order a five-frame home kit and keep your favourite for seven days before deciding.

03

Lenses ground to order

Every lens is surfaced in-house to your prescription. Three to five working days, tracked by order number.

04

Fitting

Frame heat-adjusted to your face — nose pads, temple angle, everything. Free re-fits for the life of the frame.
