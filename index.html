A Legacy Built on Trust

Bombay Opticians has been helping families see clearly since 1990. Built on generations of optical expertise and a commitment to precision, we have become one of Kumaon's most trusted optical retailers.

Today, our family proudly operates seven stores across Lalkuan, Haldwani, Almora, Pithoragarh, Didihat, Bageshwar, and Dharchula, continuing the same tradition of honesty, quality, and professional eye care.

Every customer receives personalized recommendations, accurate vision solutions, and genuine products from globally trusted brands.

Our 20-Step Precision Eye Examination

Unlike ordinary vision checks, our comprehensive 20-step eye examination evaluates your complete visual system to ensure accurate prescriptions and early detection of eye health concerns. Each step is designed with precision and care.

1
Patient Consultation
Understanding your vision concerns and lifestyle needs
2
Medical History Assessment
Reviewing past eye conditions and medications
3
Vision Screening
Initial visual acuity assessment with advanced equipment
4
Previous Prescription Analysis
Reviewing your existing glasses or lens prescription
5
Auto Refractometry
Using latest technology to measure your eye's refractive error
6
Digital Lens Measurement
Precise measurement of your vision requirements
7
Color Vision Assessment
Testing your ability to perceive colors accurately
8
Contrast Sensitivity Test
Evaluating your ability to distinguish objects from background
9
Intraocular Pressure Check
Measuring eye pressure to detect glaucoma risk
10
Binocular Vision Assessment
Testing how your eyes work together
11
Pupil Reflex Test
Checking for neurological and optical health
12
Eye Movement Evaluation
Assessing muscle coordination and eye alignment
13
Slit Lamp Examination
Detailed front and back segment analysis
14
Fundus Examination
Comprehensive retina and optic nerve evaluation
15
Depth Perception Check
Testing 3D vision and stereopsis
16
Corneal Curvature Measurement
Keratometry for contact lens fitting
17
Visual Field Screening
Detecting peripheral vision defects
18
Prescription Refinement
Fine-tuning your prescription for perfect clarity
19
Product Recommendation
Personalized suggestions based on your lifestyle
20
Prescription Counselling
Complete guidance on lens options, coatings, and care
